Garlic Prawn Udon

Home

Description

Hong Kong style udon noodles with garlic buttered prawns

Ingredients

  • 2 udon noodles 200g pre-cooked (can use egg noodles)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 headed cups of sliced cabbage
  • 1/2 Tbs soy sauce, plus 1 1/2 Tbs extra
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 200g peeled and deveined prawns
  • 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
  • 3 Tbs oyster sauce
  • 1/2 cup finely sliced spring onion
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Place noodles in a bowl of boiling water to seperate
  2. Once seperated toss with sesame oil
  3. Heat vegetable oil in wok on high heat
  4. Stir fry cabbage until charred
  5. Add 1/2 tsp sugar + 1/2 Tbs of soy sauce
  6. Fry until cabbage is coloured (approx. 1 minute)
  7. Add garlic stir fry for 20 secs
  8. Add prawns and chilli powder and stir until prawns are just cooked
  9. Add noodles, remaining soy sauce, sugar & oyster sauce
  10. Stir fry for another half minute until sauce has thickened then toss in the spring onions
